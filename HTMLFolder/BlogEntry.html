Supernatural: A 15 year old journey

What is Supernatural?

Supernatural is an American television series that aired on The CW network from 2005 to 2020. Created by Eric Kripke, it follows the story of two brothers, Sam and Dean Winchester, who hunt supernatural creatures such as demons, ghosts, and vampires. Over the course of its 15 seasons, Supernatural has gained a massive following and become one of the most popular shows on television. One of the most appealing aspects of Supernatural is its blend of genres. While the show is primarily a horror series, it also features elements of drama, comedy, and even romance. This unique combination of genres has allowed the show to appeal to a wide range of audiences, and has contributed to its longevity.

An overview of the golden seasons (1-5)

  • Season 1:

    The first season follows Sam and Dean Winchester, as they set out to find their missing father John, who is on the trail of the yellow-eyed demon who killed their mother and Sam's girlfriend. The season primarily focuses on stand-alone "monster of the week" episodes, the mysterious storyline about the yellow-eyed demon and the monster-killing gun, The Colt.

  • Season 2:

    The second season begins with Dean's encounter with death after the Winchesters get into a car accident with a demon. John decides to make a deal with the yellow-eyed demon (Azazel) to save Dean, giving up The Colt and his soul. The show begins to explore the storyline on Sam's developing power and his connection to Azazel's plan, which ultimately results in his death.

  • Season 3:

    The third season of Supernatural focuses on Sam and Dean's mission to save Dean's soul after he saves Sam and is sent to Hell in the season 2 finale. Along the way, they encounter a variety of supernatural creatures and allies, including the demon Ruby, who becomes a major character with a crucial plot twist.

  • Season 4:

    In this season we're introduced to angels as a major part of the show's mythology. The season centers around Lilith's plan to break the 66 seals that retain Lucifer in his prison and the ongoing battle between Heaven and Hell. We're also introduced to Chuck Shurley, an alleged prophet of God.

  • Season 5:

    The fifth season of Supernatural is often considered the show's peak, with many fans and critics praising it as one of the best seasons of television ever. The season brings the long-running storyline about the Winchester family and the demons to a dramatic and emotionally devastating conclusion, with the introduction of the biblical apocalypse and the arrival of the archangel Michael. Its also revealed that Chuck Shurley is actually God.

Why watch Supernatural?

Supernatural has a devoted fanbase due to its engaging storylines, dynamic characters, and blend of horror, drama, and comedy. It has also been praised for its representation of family bonds and themes of sacrifice, free will, the existence of God and redemption.
